title: "Registered users"
description: "Full access to analysis generation, favorites, and personalized recommendations"
---

Registered users are authenticated members who have completed the sign-up flow. They receive everything guests can do, plus access to the platform's core features: on-demand AI analysis generation, a personal favorites list, search history, and recommendations tuned to their taste.

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Navigate to the registration page and submit your full name, email, and password. On success, your record is inserted into the `usuarios` table with `rol = 'registrado'` and `onboarding_completo = FALSE`.

    ```sql schema.sql
    rol ENUM('invitado','registrado','admin') NOT NULL DEFAULT 'registrado'
    onboarding_completo BOOLEAN NOT NULL DEFAULT FALSE
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in and receive your JWT">
    Submit your email and password to the login endpoint. The backend validates your credentials and issues a JWT. Include this token as a `Bearer` header on all subsequent requests:

    ```http
    Authorization: Bearer <your_token>
    ```

    The JWT payload contains your user `id` and `rol`, which `authMiddleware.js` decodes on every protected request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    After your first login you are prompted to finish your cinephile profile (Step 2 of 2 in the UI). Set your username, pick your `nivel_cinefilo`, and select at least three favorite genres.

    Once you save this step, `onboarding_completo` is set to `TRUE` and your favorite genres are stored in `usuario_generos`.

    ```sql schema.sql
    nivel_cinefilo ENUM('explorador','entusiasta','experto') DEFAULT NULL
    ```

    | Level | Description |
    |-------|-------------|
    | `explorador` | Just getting started |
    | `entusiasta` | Watches regularly |
    | `experto` | Lives and breathes cinema |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enjoy full access">
    With onboarding complete you have access to all registered-user features: on-demand analysis generation, favorites, history, and personalized recommendations.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What registered users can do

### Generate AI analyses

Send a `POST` request to trigger analysis generation for any film that does not yet have a cached result:

```http
POST /api/analisis/generar
Authorization: Bearer <token>
```

The backend calls Google Gemini, stores the result in `analisis` and `capas_analisis`, and returns it. Subsequent requests for the same film are served from the database cache — no extra AI call is made.

### Save favorites

Add or remove films from your personal favorites list. Entries are stored in the `favoritos` table, keyed by your `usuario_id` and the film's `pelicula_id`.

### Search history

Every search you perform is automatically recorded in `historial_busquedas`. You can view and manage your history from your profile page.

### Personalized recommendations

The recommendations engine uses your `nivel_cinefilo` level and your preferred genres (from `usuario_generos`) to surface films that match your taste. Recommendations are visible on the Explore page once onboarding is complete.

### Complete profile management

You can update your `nivel_cinefilo`, add or remove favorite genres, and adjust your account details at any time from your profile settings.
